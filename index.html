our investment

We believe that businesses that pair capital and capability are society’s most efficient way of organizing itself at scale to solve problems – and Engro Holdings wants to be part of these businesses by investing in them. With the fundamental belief that most businesses strive to do meaningful work, Engro Holdings invests its financial capital in public and private companies across all sectors

investing for impact

Every morning, millions of people go to work to play their part in making products and services that improve the lives of those around them. We think good businesses that solve meaningful problems are a positive force for society. Engro Holdings (formerly known as Dawood Hercules Corporation) aims to participate in these businesses by providing them with the capital they need to unleash their potential and enable growth.

engro holdings creates social impact in two ways:

by investing in good businesses which unleash economic growth through the creation of jobs, income, and better goods and services for society, by giving back to communities through the philanthropic efforts of the Engro Foundation and our philanthropic partner, the dawood foundation
